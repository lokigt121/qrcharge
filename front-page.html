FAQ

How does the charging station work at my venue?
Your customers can easily rent a power bank by scanning the QR code at the station using the QrCharge app or their phone camera. They can pay either through the app or simply tap their card or phone on the terminal at the station using Apple Pay, Google Pay, or any contactless payment method. The power banks come with built-in cables for Android, iPhone, and Type-C devices, ensuring that guests can charge any phone on the go. Once they’re done, they can return the power bank to any QrCharge station.
Who handles the maintenance and repair of the stations?
We take full responsibility for the operation and maintenance of the stations. Our team ensures that the stations are always in top condition with regular maintenance and remote monitoring to prevent any technical issues. If any issue arises, we take care of it swiftly.
How quickly will issues with the station or power bank be resolved?
Our technical specialists are always available to assist your venue. If any problem occurs, our team will come to your location promptly to repair the station or power bank, ensuring minimal disruption to your service.
How many charging stations are recommended for my venue?
The number of stations you need depends on the size and layout of your venue. We assess each location individually and provide recommendations based on our experience to ensure optimal convenience for your customers.
Do you provide the necessary equipment for the station installation?
Yes, we provide everything you need. All we require from you is a power outlet, and we’ll take care of the installation, including any necessary extension cords. You won’t need to worry about a thing.
Will the charging station attract more customers to my venue?
Yes, offering free or affordable phone charging can be a great way to attract new customers. Your venue will be listed on the QrCharge map, making it visible to thousands of potential customers looking for a convenient place to charge their devices.
Is the station easy to use for my staff and customers?
Yes, the stations are designed to be intuitive and easy to use. Your customers can quickly rent a power bank either by scanning the QR code with their phone or simply tapping their card or phone on the terminal using Apple Pay, Google Pay, or any contactless payment method. There’s no need for your staff to manage the rentals—they can focus on providing excellent service.
Can I track how many people are using the charging station?
Yes, our platform provides detailed analytics, so you can track how often the station is used, how much revenue it’s generating, and even customer feedback. This allows you to make informed decisions about your service offerings.
